Exercise Manager 

Help

The exercise manager is the central point of the this application and it shows the exercises that have been chosen by your therapist for your training. There is no specific order in which the exercises have to be performed, unless your therapist has indicated otherwise. If this is the case, other exercises will be temporarily disabled until the right exercise is done.

 

Exercises are given a color, based on the category of exercises it belongs to. Green:, Red:, Yellow:, Purple:, Grey: the exercise is finished. The icon in the right bottom corner indicates the motor task that has to be performed during the exercise.

 

Pressing on an exercise in the exercise manager, leads to an information page in which the exercise is explained. From this page users can start the exercise. Results will be sent to your therapist automatically and at the end of the exercise you will return to the exercise manager.
