Help

During every trial a letter will be represented on screen. Name out loud as many words beginning with this letter as you can think of. For example if the letter “A” is shown, words that could be said are “arrow, apple, airplane, etc…”. After the minute is over, the next letter will be shown.

 

Motor task: walking (start the treadmill BEFORE starting the exercise)

 

This exercise makes use of voice recording, please speak loud and clearly. 

 

By going to the exercise manager, you are quitting the current exercise. Your results will not be saved and redoing the exercise may be required.
